Flip Inventory Features

A Simple Inventory Solution for Your Small Business.

Barcode Scanner

Quickly scan to adjust, transfer or receive items—no spreadsheets.

Track Sales & Costs

Create and assign orders (sales - purchase - production). Process item by item. We automatically update item quantities

Bill‑Of‑Materials

Assembling or producing a product ? link materials and required unit quantities, and Flip updates quantities automatically.

Smart Stock Alerts (Warning & Critical)

Warning considers committed quantities from orders; Critical triggers when on‑hand stock is below minimum.

Multi‑location

Track stock per location and transfer items in a couple of taps.

Reports

Stay ahead with curated reports for stock, sales, and costs.

Step 1

Create a product

Add set stock quantity and optional minimums. If assembling a product, also define their BOM and required quantities.

Step 2

Create orders

Purchase, sales, or production—orders give you visibility and drive automatic inventory updates when completed.

Step 3

Stay ahead

Get alerts before stockouts, adjust counts, or transfer between locations to keep operations smooth.
