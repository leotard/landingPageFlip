Prevent stockouts with smart alerts

Learn how Warning and Critical alerts help you act before items run out.

Two alert types

Flip Inventory provides Warning and Critical alerts to keep you ahead.

  • Warning: we consider quantities already committed through open orders. If on‑hand minus committed approaches your minimum, we warn you.
  • Critical: when on‑hand is below your set minimum, we raise a critical alert.

Take action fast

From an alert, you can adjust counts, create a purchase order, or transfer stock between locations to cover demand.
