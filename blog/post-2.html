From spreadsheets to Flip

A practical migration guide to accurate, low‑maintenance inventory.

Why teams outgrow spreadsheets

  • Single‑user bottlenecks: edits collide, versions drift.
  • No order workflow: hard to track what’s reserved, received, or shipped.
  • Error‑prone math: fragile formulas and hidden filters cause misses.

What you get with Flip

  • True availability: on‑hand minus committed, per location.
  • Purchase & sales orders: status‑driven updates to counts—no manual edits.
  • Smart alerts: warning/critical thresholds to prevent stockouts.

Migration in one afternoon

  1. Export your items CSV: SKU, name, location, on‑hand.
  2. Clean obvious duplicates and mismatched SKUs.
  3. Import into Flip and map columns.
  4. Set minimums for your top sellers using lead‑time + safety stock.
  5. Create your first purchase order for anything already below minimum.

Avoid these pitfalls

  • Mixed SKU formats: standardize before import; future you will thank you.
  • Stale open orders: enter any outstanding POs/SOs so availability is correct day one.
  • One giant location: set up real locations to enable fast transfers and accurate alerts.

After go‑live: keep it simple

  1. Review alerts daily; turn them into purchase orders or transfers.
  2. Revisit minimums weekly for top movers; monthly for the long tail.
  3. Archive dead SKUs quarterly to keep reports snappy.

Outcome: reliable counts, fewer emergencies, and a team that spends time selling—not fixing sheets.
